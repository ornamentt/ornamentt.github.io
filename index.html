About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at HKUST, advised by Prof. Yi R. Fung. My research focuses on multimodal large language models, web agents, multimodal reasoning, and deep research.

I am interested in building agent systems that can search, browse, reason, use tools, and learn from long-horizon trajectories. My recent work studies multimodal deep research agents, trajectory generation, reflection, self-distillation, RLVR, and large-scale SFT data for tool-use alignment.
